Other Favourites

Fontaine

Fontaine is my favourite region. It is my second "completed" region (with 100% exploration), has the best Archon Quest and World Quest (Narzissenkreuz Ordo). It's main cast is full of friendship material, characters are well defined in terms of relationships with each other and personal stories. Even fashion is used as a character defining language. Genshin's peak!

Teams

Despite having Chiori C1R1, I swear, I'm not Chiori main. With Xilonen release, the Chiori-Xilonen core is so addictive. Only the first team, Beidou Radish Power is a real preset. Others have different versions like:

  • Arlecchino Overload with Chevrouse, Fischl and Bennet/Beidou
  • Arlecchino Vape with Xingqiu, Zhongli/Kazucha, Bennet
  • Kokomi Wet Rock with Furina, Xilonen and Chiori
  • Kokomi Taser with Furina, Fishl and Beidou

Simply put, I try to use my favourite characters as much as possible.

Side note: account with less characters

While trying to figure out the team for my girlfriend's account it got pretty interesting. She feels this abyss restrictions much more then me thus we had trouble to complete it to 2 stars per each chamber.

The solution was to run Hyperbloom on the first side (Zhongli, Nahida, Xingqiu, Kuki Shinobu) with the Burnvape on second side: Mualani, Mavuika, Xilonen and Collei. Collei was only level 20, we got her some random Emblem artifacts to make her burst consistently and help keep the burning reaaction for the Tenebrous Papilla (which works wonders) and have bow for the Aeonblight Drake. Xingqiu was suprisingly good on the first side where his orbitals were enough to break crystals dropped by Holawaqa Ngoubou (Natlan dragon).

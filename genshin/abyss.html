5.5 April 2025 - Freeze is quite fun, actually

Despite Chasca beeing called by the community a Pyro DPS I really like her in Freeze teams. Escoffier premiere would definitely be impactful for this team - currently I either use Kokomi or Qiqi - both are mostly there to ensure high level of Furina Fanfere. With Escoffier this team not only would have great levels of Fanfere, more resistance shred and additional source of off-field damage.

As for Arlecchino team, it's my standard comfy team. I also tried similar Pyro-Geo team with Navia.

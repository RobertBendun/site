Ichcahuipilli's Aegis

Achivement I, II, III

Defeat Local Legend: Ichcahuipilli's Aegis.

During the course of completing a single challenge, defeat 10 Nightsoul Warriors during the Trial of Decisions.

Defeat Ichcahuipilli's Aegis without being hit by any attacks.

Draining HP - let's have Furina to benefit from changing HP and healers. I present to you the most comfy team in Genshin Impact:

Two healers allow for maximum Curse stacks from the enemy without breaking a sweat. Xilonen resistance shred and DMG buff provides support for all of the characters, Kokomi ensures that everybody stays alive and deals some nice dmg herself.
